کونیفۆرم

'کونیفۆرم' (Cuneiform) is a Historical Fiction novel written originally in Kurdish by Kurdish-American author A.K. al-Abad. The novel is set during the beginning decades of the Ottomon Empire and details the fall and destruction of the Southern Region of the fictional Ottomon-esque Khanate 'al-Rwal.'
