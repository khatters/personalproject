'کونیفۆرم'

'کونیفۆرم' (Cuneiform) is a Historical Fiction novel set during the beginning decades of the Abbasid Empire and details the fall and destruction of the Southern Region of the fictional Abbasid-esque Khanate 'al-Rwal.'
